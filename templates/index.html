LinkedIn Profile Scraper

Scrape Sales Navigator search results and export to CSV

How to get it: open linkedin.com in Chrome → press F12 → Application tab → Cookies → https://www.linkedin.com → find li_at → copy the Value. This is your login token — keep it private.

Apply your filters on LinkedIn or Sales Navigator, copy the URL from your browser, and paste it above.

Each page has ~25 results (Sales Nav) or ~10 results (regular search).

Sheet must be shared as "Anyone with the link — Viewer". Put Sales Nav search URLs in column A, one per row. Header row is optional.

CSV must have Sales Nav search URLs in column A, one per row. Header row optional.

Maximum profiles to collect from each search URL. Internally converts to pages: ceil(N ÷ 25).
